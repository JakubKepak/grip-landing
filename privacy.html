Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how Grip handles your data.

Summary: Grip is designed with privacy in mind. All your workout data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Grip is a privacy-focused app. We collect minimal information:

  • No personal data: We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information through the app.
  • No account required: You can use Grip without creating an account or signing in.
  • Local storage only: All your workouts, exercises, and progress data are stored locally on your device.

2. How Your Data is Stored

All data you create in Grip—including workout templates, completed sessions, calendar entries, and custom exercises—is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage.

This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • We cannot access your workout data
  • Your data is not uploaded to any servers

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone.

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to share with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Grip does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media trackers
  • Any other third-party data collection services

5. Data Backup

Your workout data may be included in your device's standard backup (such as iCloud backup on iOS). This is handled by your device's operating system and is subject to Apple's privacy policies.

6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it:

  • Delete individual workouts or exercises within the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all Grip data from your device

7. Children's Privacy

Grip does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we don't collect personal data from any users, this applies to users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date below.
